How We Assist You During Claims (Cashless & Reimbursement)

We guide you through the two types of claims to ensure a smooth and quick settlement process.

Cashless Claim Process

  1. Inform the insurer/TPA 48 hours before planned admission.
  2. Submit the pre-authorization form at the network hospital.
  3. Hospital and Insurer handle settlement directly.
  4. UMM's Role: We monitor the approval status to prevent delays.

Reimbursement Claim Process

  1. Pay all hospital bills yourself upon discharge.
  2. Collect all original bills, reports, and discharge summary.
  3. Submit all documentation to the insurer within the specified timeframe.
  4. UMM's Role: We assist in compiling and submitting the correct documentation to ensure zero rejection.
