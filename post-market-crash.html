Market Crash Alert: Should You Stop Your SIP?

Market la **periya volatility** irukkum bodhu, **SIPs stop** pannanum nu nenaikkiradhu **naturally** dhaan varum. Aana, **successful wealth creation** ku, indha **panic** ah eppadi manage pannanum nu theriyanum. Stop panradhukku badhila, neenga innum neraiya units ah low price la vaangalaam.

The Investor's Biggest Enemy: Emotion (Fear of Missing Out)

Oru market crash, unga **portfolio** value ah konjam **kammi** pannum. Ippadi time la, **Fear of Loss** dhaan **panic selling** ah create pannum. Namma **SIP** la irukkuradhukku kaaranam, emotional decisions ah avoid panni, **discipline** ah maintain panradhukku dhaan.

Takeaway: Investing is a marathon, not a sprint. Market ups and downs are normal. Stopping your SIP during a dip converts a temporary loss into a permanent one.

How Rupee Cost Averaging (RCA) Works in Your Favor

SIP oda **most powerful benefit** dhaan **RCA**. Market crash aagum bodhu, unga **fixed SIP amount** ku **neraiya mutual fund units** ah vaangalaam. Average purchase cost ah korachchu, market thiruppi mela varum bodhu, unga **overall return** ah **boost** pannum.

The Strategy: When to Stop, When to Start

The Only Time to Stop Your SIP

Neenga SIP ah stop panna vendiya situation:

  • Goal is Near: Unga financial goal (E.g., house down payment) **next 1-2 years** la irundha, unga money ah safe debt funds ku shift pannanum.
  • Genuine Financial Emergency: Unga **emergency fund** theendhuttuchu, and next SIP panna mudiyala.

Why a Market Crash is the Best Time to Increase SIP

Market crash na, discount sale nu artham da. Unga portfolio la **cash balance** irundha, indha time ah use panni, **lumpsum** ah kooda invest panna paarkalaam. Namma SIP amount ah konjam **increase** panna, low price la **extra units** vaanga mudiyum.

Post-Crash Checklist (UMM Guidance)

Market down aagum bodhu, panic aagaama indha **moondru things** ah check pannunga:

  • Review Goal Timeline: Unga goal innum **5 years** ku mela irukkaa? Irundha, **relax**! Time is on your side.
  • Check Asset Allocation: Unga portfolio la **equity** romba kammiya poyirukka? MFD kooda serndhu **rebalance** pannunga.
  • Emergency Fund: Unga **6 months expenses** safe ah irukkaa? Irundha, you are protected.
