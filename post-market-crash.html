Past Crashes and Recovery Time

History-la 2 periya crashes (2008 and 2020) nadandhirukku:

  • 2008 (Global Financial Crisis): Market 50% drop aanalum, 3 years-la full-a recover aagi, new high-a achieve pannuchu.
  • 2020 (Pandemic): Market 35% drop aanalum, 1 year-kku ulla full-a recover aagiduchu.

The Point: Nee crash-a ignore panni, SIP-a continue panninaa, history unga side-la dhaan irukku.
