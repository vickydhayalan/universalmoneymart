Mastering the Mutual Fund Factsheet: Decoding Alpha, Beta, and Portfolio Turnover

Oru fund oda **factsheet** dhaan adhu **eppadi perform pannudhu** nu sollum. Idhu **technical terms** (ratios) la irukkum. Fund Selection ah correct ah panna, indha **terms** ah **eppadi read** pannanum nu theriyanum.

Section 1: The Basics (NAV, AUM, Exit Load)

Factsheet oda **first page** la irukka basic terms:

  • NAV (Net Asset Value): Oru unit oda current value.
  • AUM (Assets Under Management): Fund manage panra **total money** (size of the fund).
  • Expense Ratio: Fund manage panna edukkira **annual fee**. Direct Plan vida Regular Plan la **konjam athigam** irukkum.

Section 2: The Core Ratios (Understanding Performance)

Idhudhaan **fund manager** evalo **nalla work** pannirukkar nu kaattum. Idhu **benchmark** kooda compare panni dhaan paakkanum.

Alpha: Generating Outperformance

Alpha na, **fund manager** benchmark (e.g., Nifty 50) **vida** evalo **extra return** generate pannirukkar nu artham. **Positive Alpha** dhaan **best**. Positive Alpha na, fund manager **skill** ah use pannirukkar nu artham.

Beta: Measuring Volatility (Risk)

Beta na, fund **market volatility** ku evalo **sensitive** ah irukku nu sollum. **Beta > 1** (E.g., 1.2) na, market 10% mela pona, indha fund **12% mela pogum**. Risk kooda athigam.

Sharpe Ratio: Risk-Adjusted Returns

Sharpe Ratio dhaan **risk eduthadhukku** evalo **return** kidaichirukku nu kaattum. Higher Sharpe Ratio dhaan **best**.

  • Example: Fund A (15% return, Sharpe 1.0) Fund B (18% return, Sharpe 0.8). Fund A dhaan **safe and better** choice.

Section 3: Portfolio Turnover (Strategy Check)

Portfolio Turnover na, oru varusham la **fund manager** evalo **stocks ah vaangi/vitrukkanga** nu sollum. **High turnover** na, fund manager **short-term trading** ah namburaar nu artham.

Factsheet Analysis Help Venuma?

Indha technical terms ah analysis panna, unga advisor kooda connect pannunga.
