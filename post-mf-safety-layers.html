The Safety Checks: Understanding the Roles of the Trustee, Custodian, and Auditor

Mutual Fund la **money** podum bodhu, unga **money** ah **AMC** (Asset Management Company) dhaan **invest** pannum. Aana, **AMC** thappa pannaama irukka, **moondru independent bodies** dhaan **monitor** pannum. Idhudhaan SEBI create panna safety layers da.

Layer 1: The Trustee (The Investor's Watchdog)

Trustee dhaan **highest authority** da. Trustee oda **main duty**, **investors** oda **interest** ah **protect** panradhu dhaan. Idhu **AMC** ah **direct ah supervise** pannum.

Key Responsibilities of the Trustee:

  • Supervision: AMC **SEBI rules** ah **sariyaana maathiri** follow pannudha nu monitor panradhu.
  • Compliance: AMC oda **ellam operations** ah **regular ah** check panradhu.
  • Fund Manager Change: **Trustee** ku **Fund Manager** ah **fire** panna kooda **power** irukku.

Layer 2: The Custodian (The Asset Keeper)

Custodian na, **safety deposit box** maathiri dhaan. Unga **money** ku vaangina **stocks, bonds, and assets** ah **safe** ah vechchirukkum **separate bank** or financial institution dhaan.

Why is the Custodian Important?

Custodian and **AMC** **rendu company** ah irukkuradhunaala, **AMC** ku unga **assets** ah **misuse** panna **chance illa** da. Idhu dhaan **periya safety layer**.

Layer 3: The Auditor (The Financial Validator)

Auditor dhaan **endha thappum** nadakkala nu **validate** pannum. Idhu **financial expert** ah irukkum.

Auditor's Role in Transparency

  • NAV Validation: **NAV calculation** ah **sariyaana maathiri** panni irukkaanga nu check panradhu.
  • Annual Accounts: Fund oda **annual accounts** ah **verify** panni, **investors** ku **clear report** ah kudukkum.

Conclusion: Trust in the System

Conclusion: Unga money **oru aal** (Fund Manager) kitta mattum illa da. **Trustee, Custodian, and Auditor** maathiri **moondru independent** and **regulated bodies** dhaan unga **investments** ah **protect** pannum. Idhu dhaan **SEBI** oda **investor protection structure**.
