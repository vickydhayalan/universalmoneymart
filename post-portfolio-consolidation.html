Simplify Your Portfolio: Understanding CAMS, KFintech, and KYC Consolidation

Oru investor **multiple funds** la **money** podum bodhu, **different folios** and **statements** ah manage pannanum. Idhu romba **complex** ah irukkum. Portfolio Consolidation dhaan idhukku **solution** da.

The Problem of Scattered Investments (Admin Headache)

Neenga **HDFC**, **ICICI**, **Parag Parikh** maathiri neraiya **AMC** la invest pannina, unga **statements** ellam **separate ah** varum. Idhu **performance** ah **accurate ah** track panna **thadai** ah irukkum. UMM maathiri oru MFD dhaan, **oru view** la **ellam investments** ah kaattum.

The Solution: Consolidating with CAMS and KFintech (RTAs)

Mutual Fund industry la, **RTAs (Registrar and Transfer Agents)** dhaan **record-keeping** ah paathukkum. CAMS and KFintech dhaan **main RTAs** da.

RTA Manages These Funds (Example) Service Focus
CAMS HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Aditya Birla Sun Life Investor Services & Data Management
KFintech Kotak, Axis, Tata, Mirae Asset Investor Services & Transaction Processing

KYC Status: The Non-Negotiable Compliance Check

KYC (Know Your Customer) **complete** ah irundha dhaan, neenga **neraiya funds** la **invest** panna mudiyum. Oru vaati **KYC complete** aana, adhu **industry-wide** ah **valid** aagum.

UMM's Role in Administrative Ease: Your MFD helps you consolidate your portfolio data into **one single platform view**. Adhu dhaan **performance tracking** ku **simple** ah irukkum.

Administrative Strategy: How to Simplify

  • **One View:** Always ask your MFD for **one consolidated statement** that shows **ellam AMC** oda performance.
  • **KYC Update:** Address change aana, udane **KYC** ah **update** pannunga.
  • **Nomination:** Ellam folios laiyum **nomination** ah **update** pannirukkanu check pannunga.
