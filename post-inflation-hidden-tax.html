Inflation: The Hidden Tax on Your Savings (And How Equity Investing Helps)

Income Tax pathi ellarum pesuvanga, aana **Inflation** dhaan **hidden ah** unga money ah **azhikkum** (erode pannum) **tax** da. Inflation na, **goods and services** oda **price increase** aaguradhu dhaan. Idhai beat panna strategy venum.

What is Inflation? (The Erosion of Purchasing Power)

Simple ah sollanumna, **indha varusham ₹100 ku vaangina oru porul**, **next year** adhe porul ah vaanga **₹105** thevai aagum. Andha **₹5 difference** dhaan **Inflation** da.

Real Value Loss: Unga money oda **purchasing power** koraindha, adhu **negative return** ah kudukkum nu artham.

The Problem with Cash and Fixed Deposits

Neraiya peru **Bank FD** or **savings account** la money vechcha, adhu **safe** nu nenaikkiranga. Aana, **inflation** ah paatha, adhu safe illa da.

  • **FDs Lose:** Average **FD return** 7% ah irundhaalum, **inflation** 6% irundha, unga **actual profit** (Real Return) **1%** dhaan da. Taxum pay panna vendum.
  • **Cash Loses Everything:** Savings account la irukka money, **inflation** ku **full ah** lose aagum.

The Solution: Generating Real Returns

Real Return na, unga **Investment Return** (E.g., 12%) ah **Inflation** (E.g., 6%) ku **mela** irundha dhaan **positive** ah irukkum. Idhudhaan **long-term wealth** create panna mukkiyam.

The Role of Equity & Mutual Funds

Historically paaththa, **Equity** dhaan **long run** la **inflation** ah nalla beat pannirukku. Idhudhaan **SIP** use panni **Equity Mutual Funds** la invest panradhukku **main kaaranam**:

  • Growth Potential: Companies oda **profits** inflation ku yetha maathiri increase aagum, adhunaala **stock price** kooda increase aagum.
  • SIP Discipline: **SIP** use panni **low risk** la **equity** ah vaangalaam.

Final Strategy: Allocation is Key

Unga **Financial Goals** ku yetha maathiri, money ah **divide** pannanum:

  • Short-Term Money (1-3 years): **Safety** mukkiyam. Debt Funds / Fixed Deposits (Accept low real returns).
  • Long-Term Money (5+ years): **Growth** mukkiyam. Equity Mutual Funds (SIP) for generating high **real returns**.
