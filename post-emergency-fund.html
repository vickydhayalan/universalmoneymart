**SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)** start panradhukku munnadi, unga **Financial House**-kku oru **strong foundation** venum da. Adhu dhaan **Emergency Fund**.

**Emergency Fund** na enna? **Unpredictable expenses** (job loss, sudden medical bill, car accident) varum bodhu, unga **SIP**-a **break** pannaama, **long-term growth**-a **protect** panra **safety cushion** dhaan. Indha fund illai-na, nee **loss**-la kooda **investment**-a sell panna vendi varum.

Nee **minimum 6 months**-kku venumana **total monthly expenses**-a dhaan save pannanum. Idhula **rent, EMI, grocery, transport** maadhiri **basic expenses** mattum dhaan include aaganum. **Luxury expenses** vendaam.

III. ğŸ—ï¸ Where to Keep This Money? (Safety First!)

Idhu **Emergency Fund**. Adhunaala **long-term growth**-a vida **safety** and **liquidity** dhaan romba mukkiyam. **Stock Market**-la **kandippa podave koodadhu**.

Best Options for Safety & Access

Unga **money**-a indha **3 options**-la dhaan vekkanum:

  • **Savings Bank Account:** **Easy access**-kku konjam amount inga vechukalam.
  • **Liquid Funds:** **Safety** romba jaasthi, plus **1-day-la withdrawal** panna mudiyum. Idhu dhaan best choice.
  • **Short-term Fixed Deposits (FDs):** **6 months** or **1-year FD** pottu, konjam **interest** kidaikkum.

The Big Mistake

**Market crash** aagum bodhu (stock price low-a irukkum), ungalukku **money** venum-na, nee **loss**-la irukkura **investment units**-a **sell** panna vendi varum. Idhu unga **long-term growth**-a **destroy** pannidum.

Solution: Protection

**Emergency Fund** irundhaa, **market** eppdi irundhaalum, nee **SIP**-a **continue** panna laam, and **long-term goal**-a **protect** panna laam.
