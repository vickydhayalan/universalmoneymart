SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) start panradhukku munnadi, unga Financial House-kku oru strong foundation venum da. Adhu dhaan Emergency Fund.

Emergency Fund na enna? Unpredictable expenses (job loss, sudden medical bill, car accident) varum bodhu, unga SIP-a **break** pannaama, long-term growth-a protect panra **safety cushion** dhaan. Indha fund illai-na, nee loss-la kooda investment-a sell panna vendi varum.

Best Options for Safety & Access (Low Risk)

Unga money-a indha 3 options-la dhaan vekkanum:

  • Savings Bank Account: **Easy access**-kku konjam amount inga vechukalam.
  • Liquid Funds: Safety romba jaasthi, plus **1-day-la withdrawal** panna mudiyum. Idhu dhaan **MFD**-s suggest panra **best choice**.
  • Short-term Fixed Deposits (FDs): 6 months or 1-year FD pottu, konjam interest kidaikkum.
