Debt Fund Risks: Understanding Interest Rate Risk and Credit Risk

Neraiya investors **Debt Funds** ah **risk-free** nu nenaikiraanga. Aana, adhu **sariyaana maathiri** illa da. Debt Funds la **rendu main risk** irukku. Idhai purinjukitta dhaan, unga **Emergency Fund** ah **sariyaana fund** la podanum.

The Myth of Zero Risk (Debt is Not Bank FD)

Bank Fixed Deposit ku **Deposit Insurance** (DICGC) irukku, adhaala adhu **safe**. Aana, Debt Funds ah **AMC** (Asset Management Company) dhaan manage pannum. Idhula **NAV fluctuation** vara chance irukku.

Statutory Warning: Debt funds can and do lose value. They are not guaranteed or capital-protected by the AMC or the government.

Risk 1: Interest Rate Risk (The NAV Fluctuation)

Idhu **market risk** ah based pannum. **RBI** interest rates ah **change** pannum bodhu, indha risk varum:

How it Works

  • Rule: Interest rates **mela** pona, **existing bond prices** **kela** pogum.
  • Result: Bond price kela pona, Debt Fund oda **NAV kooda kela pogum** da.
  • Duration: **Long-term Debt Funds** ku dhaan indha risk **romba athigam** irukkum.

Risk 2: Credit Risk (The Default Factor)

Idhudhaan **borrower (company)** money ah **thiruppi kudukkaama** irukkum risk. Debt funds, **companies** ku loan kudukkum. Andha company **default** aana, unga **fund value** kooda **kela pogum**.

Importance of Credit Rating

Funds oda **risk** ah paakka, **Credit Rating** ah check pannanum. AAA rated bonds dhaan **lowest risk**. Lower rated (e.g., AA or below) bonds **high return** kudukkum, aana **risk** kooda **athigam** irukkum.

  • High Quality: Emergency Fund ku, **high-rated (AAA)** and **PSU** bonds la invest panra funds ah dhaan select pannanum.

Strategy: How to Mitigate These Risks

Mitigation Strategy dhaan mukkiyam. Unga MFD ah kettu indha strategy ah follow pannunga:

  • Avoid Long Duration: **Interest Rate Risk** ah kammi panna, **Ultra Short Duration** or **Liquid Funds** ah dhaan use pannanum.
  • Check Credit Quality: Fund portfolio la **90% ku mela AAA rated** bonds dhaan irukkanu check pannunga.
