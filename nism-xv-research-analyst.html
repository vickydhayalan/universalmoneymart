III. üìä Core Skill 1: **Company Valuation** (Stock Analysis)

Research Analyst-oda **main velai** dhaan **stock price** **correct-a** irukkaa nu **analysis** panradhu.

Fundamental Analysis vs. Technical Analysis

Fundamental Analysis: Company-oda **Balance Sheet**, **Profit/Loss (P&L)**, **Management quality** pathi analysis panradhu. Indha section-la dhaan **jaasthi questions** varum.

Technical Analysis: **Chart movements** and **historical price trends** pathi analysis panradhu.

Financial Statement Analysis

Financial Statement analysis-la **Ratios** (e.g., **P/E Ratio**, **Debt-to-Equity Ratio**) pathi nalla purinjikkanum. Idhukkum **NAV calculation** maadhiri **sum questions** varum.

IV. üí° Core Skill 2: Research Report Writing

NISM exam **ethics**-kku romba mukkiyam. **Research Report** ezhudhum bodhu **SEBI rules**-a follow pannanum.

Ethical Research Report Ezhudha Tips

  • Disclosure of Interest: Nee oru stock-a **recommend** panradhukku munnadi, **ungalukkum andha stock-la investment irukkaa** nu **kandippa** sollanum.
  • Rating System: **Buy, Sell, Hold** rating eppdi kudukkanum nu **clear process** irukku. Adha study pannanum.
  • Public Appearance: **TV show** illa **social media**-la **comment** pannum bodhu **enna rules**-a follow pannanum.
