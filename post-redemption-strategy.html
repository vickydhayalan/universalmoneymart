Lumpsum Withdrawal vs. SWP: The Smart Way to Take Money Out of Mutual Funds

Retirement ku appuram, unga **investments** ah **eppadi edukkardhu** nu theriyanum. Lumpsum withdrawal (oru vaati periya amount edukkardhu) or SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) la irundhu **sariyaana choice** dhaan unga **corpus longevity** ah decide pannum.

The Core Distinction: SWP vs Lumpsum

SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)

SWP na, unga **corpus** la irundhu **fixed amount** ah **monthly ah** edukkardhu dhaan. Idhu **passive income** ah create pannum.

Lumpsum Withdrawal

Lumpsum Withdrawal na, **oru vaati** ah unga **full amount** ah illa **periya part** ah eduthu veliya kondu varradhu dhaan. Idhula **risk** romba athigam.

Lumpsum Withdrawal: The Timing Risk (When to Avoid It)

Market **down** ah irukkum bodhu, neenga **lumpsum** ah edutha, **periya loss** vara chance irukku. Idhudhaan **market timing risk**.

Why Lumpsum Harms Your Corpus

Neenga **oru vaati** ah **neraiya units** ah sell pannuveenga. Andha units **market low** la irundhaalum, neenga **forced ah** edukkira dhu dhaan **periya financial damage**.

SWP: Creating a Paycheck from Your Corpus

SWP dhaan **retirement** ku **ideal solution** da. Idhukku **moondru periya benefits** irukku:

  • Lower Tax Liability: Neenga **konjam units** ah mattum dhaan sell pannuveenga. Tax **kammiya** irukkum.
  • Corpus Preservation: Remaining money **market la invest** ah irukkum. Adhu **grow** aagum, unga **corpus longevity** ah increase pannum.
  • Discipline & Predictability: Monthly **fixed income** varum. Budget plan panna **easy** ah irukkum.

The Tax Impact (LTCG vs STCG)

Redemption (sell panradhu) ku **tax** apply aagum. Neenga **sariyaana units** ah select pannanum:

Tax Alert: **Term Insurance** maathiri **long-term units** ah (LTCG) dhaan **first** redeem panna paakkanum. **Short-term units** (STCG) ku **tax** romba athigam.

SWP la, unga MFD **smart ah** units ah select panni, **minimum tax** ah pay panna help pannuvanga.
