I. üéØ Introduction: Why Derivatives Exam?

Derivatives pathi **legal knowledge** venum-na, indha **NISM Series VIII** dhaan **mandatory**. Indha exam **exchange**-la **trading** panna vendiyavangalukku dhaan mukkiyam.

III. üìä Core Skill 1: **Futures & Options** (The Basics)

Derivatives-la **Futures** and **Options** dhaan **basic building block**-a irukku.

Futures Contracts: The Obligation

Futures na **kandippa** (obligation) **future date**-la oru **price**-kku **buy** illa **sell** pannanum. **Risk** romba jaasthi.

Options Contracts: The Right, Not the Obligation

Options na **right** mattum dhaan irukkum. Nee **buy** illa **sell** panna **vendiyadhu illa**. Adhunaala **risk** konjam **manage** panna laam.

Key Calculations

Exam-la **Margin requirements** (ethanai money advance-a vekkanum), **Premium/Strike Price** calculations, and **Option Greeks** (Delta, Theta) pathi **kandippa** questions varum. Idhukku **maths** nalla practice venum.

IV. üí° Core Skill 2: **Trading Strategies** and Hedging

**Derivatives**-a **risk**-kku use panna koodadhu. Adha **risk management**-kku dhaan mukkiyamaana purpose.

Hedging (Risk Protection)

Hedging na, unga **existing stock investment**-a **protect** panna **Options/Futures** use panradhu. Example-kku, unga stock price down aanaalum, **Options** unga **loss**-a **cover** pannum.

Exam-la **Bear Spreads** and **Bull Spreads** maadhiri **trading strategies**-m padikkanum.
