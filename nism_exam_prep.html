Certification Requirements for Mutual Fund Distributors

To operate in the Indian financial market, regulatory compliance through NISM certification is mandatory. We focus on the core modules essential for mutual fund advisory.

NISM Series V-A (Mandatory MFD)

This certifies your fundamental knowledge of the Mutual Fund industry, schemes, legal structure, and compliance rules. **Passing Score: 50%**.

UMM's Credibility

Renewal Cycle

The certification is valid for 3 years. Renewal must be done either by re-taking the exam or through a Continuing Professional Education (CPE) program.

NISM V-A Exam Breakdown (100 Questions)

Understanding the weightage helps you prioritize your study time effectively.

Topic Area Weightage Focus
Basic Concepts & Structures 20% Types of funds, SEBI/AMFI regulatory roles.

UMM's 3-Step Strategy to Pass the NISM Exam

Step 1: Focus on Core Concepts

Don't jump straight into questions. Read the official NISM workbook (or a good substitute) at least twice. Master KYC/Compliance basics first.

Step 2: Timed Mock Tests

Practice is everything. Attempt at least 5-7 full-length mock tests under strict time limits to build speed and stamina. Aim for 70%+ score in mocks.

Step 3: Review Mistakes & Revisit

Analyze every wrong answer in your mock tests. Focus on the low-scoring syllabus sections and read the theory again before re-testing.
