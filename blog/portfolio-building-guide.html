3. Layer 1 — Emergency Fund First!

⚠️ Why First?

Emergency-ல investments sell பண்ணா wrong time exit. Market bottom-ல forced sell = permanent loss! Emergency Fund = Protection shield for investments.

  • Amount: Monthly expenses × 3-6 months
  • Where: Liquid Fund (6-7% + T+1)
  • Separate account — don't mix with investments
  • Build this BEFORE starting any SIP!

8. Rebalancing — When + How

Target allocation-ல இருந்து drift ஆனா back to original ratio adjust பண்றது.

📐 Example

Target: 70% Equity + 30% Debt. After bull run: 80-20. Rebalance: Sell some equity, buy debt → Back to 70-30.

  • When: Annual (March end ideal)
  • Trigger: If allocation drifts > 5%
  • Life events: Marriage, salary hike, new goal

9. Common Portfolio Mistakes

  • 🚫 Over-diversification (10-15 funds) — 3-5 funds max!
  • 🚫 No emergency fund before SIP — Emergency first always
  • 🚫 Ignoring rebalancing — Annual reset mandatory
  • 🚫 SIP stop during market fall — Continue always! Cost averaging!
