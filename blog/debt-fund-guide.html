1. Debt Fund என்னன்னு?

Debt Fund என்பது உன் பணத்தை Government Bonds, Corporate Bonds, மற்றும் Fixed Income securities-ல invest பண்ற Mutual Fund. Government அல்லது Companies-க்கு loan கொடுக்கறா மாதிரி, அதுக்கு பதிலா Fixed Interest income கிடைக்கும்.

⚙️ How it Works

நீ Fund-ல invest பண்றாய் → Fund Manager Bonds வாங்குறார் → Interest Income வருது → NAV slowly grow ஆகும். Equity-ஐ விட stable, predictable returns.

3. Liquid Funds — Emergency Fund Parking

91 நாட்கள் வரை maturity உள்ள money market instruments-ல invest. Emergency Fund, Short-term parking, STP source fund-க்கு best.

💧 Liquid Fund Stats

Risk: Very Low
Returns: 6-7% CAGR
Liquidity: T+1 day
Best for: Emergency fund
Min Hold: No lock-in
💡 Smart Tip

Bank Savings 3-4% கொடுக்கும். Liquid Fund 6-7% கொடுக்கும். Salary credit ஆன உடனே excess பணத்தை Liquid Fund-ல park பண்ணுங்க!

4. Short Duration Funds — 1-3 Year Goals

1 to 3 years maturity bonds-ல invest. Car purchase, house renovation மாதிரி specific timeline goals-க்கு perfect.

📅 Short Duration Stats

Risk: Low
Returns: 6.5-7.5% CAGR
Rate Risk: Moderate
Best for: 1-3 year goals
Min Hold: 1-3 Years

5. Corporate Bond Funds — FD Alternative

Minimum 80% AAA-rated companies-ஓட bonds-ல invest (SEBI rule). Tata, Reliance, HDFC போன்ற high-quality companies.

🏢 Corporate Bond Stats

Risk: Low-Medium
Returns: 7-8% CAGR
Credit: 80%+ AAA mandatory
Best for: Conservative, 2-4 yrs
Min Hold: 2-4 Years

6. Gilt Funds — Government Securities

Indian Government bonds-ல மட்டும் invest. Default risk ZERO. ஆனா Interest Rate Risk உண்டு — rate ஏறினா NAV down, குறைஞ்சா NAV up.

🏛️ Gilt Fund Stats

Risk: Low (Credit), Medium (Rate)
Returns: 6-8% CAGR
Default Risk: ZERO
Best for: Conservative investors
Min Hold: 3-5 Years

7. Credit Risk Funds — Higher Risk Debt

AA-rated அல்லது அதுக்கு கீழ் rated bonds. Higher returns, ஆனா default possibility real.

⚠️ Caution

2019-2020-ல சில Credit Risk funds NAV crash பண்ணினது நினைவில் கொள்ளுங்க. Beginners-க்கு recommend பண்ண மாட்டேன்.

⚠️ Credit Risk Stats

Risk: Medium-High
Returns: 8-10%+ CAGR
Default Risk: Real possibility
Best for: Experienced investors
Min Hold: 3+ Years

9. Interest Rate Risk

Market interest rates change ஆனா, bond prices opposite direction-ல move ஆகும்.

  • Interest Rate குறைஞ்சா → Bond Prices ஏறும் → NAV Up
  • Interest Rate ஏறினா → Bond Prices குறையும் → NAV Down
📌 Rule

Longer duration funds (Gilt) = More rate sensitivity. Liquid Fund = Very low sensitivity. Choose duration based on your goal timeline.

10. Tax Guide — 2023 Rule Change

⚠️ Important Update

April 2023-க்கு பின் purchase பண்ண Debt Funds-ல, Indexation benefit REMOVED. Holding period எவ்வளவு இருந்தாலும், Gains உங்க Income Tax Slab Rate-ல tax ஆகும்.

Example: ₹2,00,000 gain, 30% tax bracket → ₹60,000 tax (no indexation benefit).

13. FAQ

Q: Debt Fund 100% safe-ஆ?

✅ Answer

Liquid, Short Duration, Gilt Funds — relatively safe. Credit Risk Funds-ல default risk உண்டு. Fund type-ஐ பொருத்து risk vary ஆகும்.

Q: Emergency Fund எங்க வைக்கணும்?

Liquid Fund best choice. T+1 day-ல access, low risk, bank savings-ஐ விட better returns.
