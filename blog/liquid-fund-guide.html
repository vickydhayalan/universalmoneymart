5. T+1 Redemption — How Fast?

  • Monday before 2 PM — Request submit
  • Tuesday — Money in bank account!
⚡ Cut-off Time

2:00 PM cut-off. Before 2 PM → next working day money. After 2 PM → 2 working days. Holiday இருந்தா next working day count ஆகும்.

6. Emergency Fund Strategy

How much? Salaried: 3-6 months expenses. Freelancer/Business: 6-12 months.

Example: Monthly expenses ₹50,000 → Emergency Fund ₹1.5L to ₹3L in Liquid Fund.

✅ Why Liquid Fund and Not Bank?

6-7% returns vs 3-4% savings + T+1 access (practically same as savings) + No minimum balance = Best emergency fund vehicle!

7. STP Strategy — Liquid to Equity

Bonus/Windfall received? Direct equity-ல போடாதீங்க. STP strategy use பண்ணுங்க:

  1. ₹5L bonus → Liquid Fund-ல park (6.5% earn ஆரம்பிக்கும்)
  2. Every month ₹50,000 STP → Equity Fund
  3. 10 months-ல full amount equity-ல invested
  4. Bonus: Liquid Fund income ≈ ₹15,000-20,000 extra!
💡 Why STP?

Cost averaging benefit. Market timing risk avoid. Liquid fund earns while waiting. Especially powerful in volatile markets!

8. யார் Use பண்ணணும்?

  • 🚨 Emergency Fund — 3-6 months expenses
  • 💰 Salary Surplus — Monthly leftover savings
  • 🔄 STP Source — Lumpsum → Equity gradually
  • 🏦 FD Mature Amount — Next decision wait
  • ⏰ Short-term Goal (<3 months) — Upcoming expense

9. Risks — Honest Truth

  • Interest Rate Risk — Very low, almost negligible
  • Credit Risk — SEBI strict regulations, top-rated instruments
  • NAV Drop Risk — Extremely rare (2008 type events)
  • Returns Not Guaranteed — 6-7% indicative, not fixed like FD
