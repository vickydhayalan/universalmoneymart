6. SIP vs Lumpsum in ELSS

SIP — Monthly invest, cost averaging benefit. Every installment-க்கும் separate 3-year lock-in.

Lumpsum — March-ல one-time. Single lock-in. Market timing risk.

💡 Important Nuance

SIP-ல ஒவ்வொரு installment-க்கும் separate 3-year lock-in. Jan 2024 installment — unlocks Jan 2027. Feb 2024 — unlocks Feb 2027. Plan பண்ணி invest பண்ணுங்க!

  • 3+ years invest பண்ண ready
  • Equity risk accept பண்றவங்க

10. Common Mistakes

  • March rush-ல panic invest — Year-end lumpsum risk. Solution: April-யே SIP start.
  • 3 years-லயே immediately redeem — Wealth destroy. Solution: Goal-based redeem.
  • Emergency Fund இல்லாம start — Lock-in funds stuck. Solution: Emergency first.
  • Multiple ELSS overlap — Same stocks. Solution: 1-2 funds போதும்.
