1. Redemption என்னன்னு?

Mutual Fund units-ஐ NAV price-ல sell பண்ணி cash-ஆ bank-ல பெறுவது.

⚡ Key Facts

Equity: T+2 days · Debt/Liquid: T+1 day · Cut-off: 3:00 PM · Full or Partial · Tax applicable

2. Good Reasons to Redeem ✅

  • 🎯 Goal Achieved — Target amount reach! House, education complete.
  • 🔄 Better Fund — Consistent underperformance, better option found.
  • ⚖️ Rebalancing — Allocation drift > 5%. Partial redeem to rebalance.
  • 🚨 Genuine Emergency — Emergency fund exhausted. Last resort.
  • 📅 Life Event — Retirement, marriage — planned exit.

3. Bad Reasons — Never Exit! ❌

❌ Never Do These

Panic sell (market down) · News/rumor-based exit · Early profit booking · NAV too high misconception · Short-term (1 yr) underperformance

7. LTCG ₹1.25L Exemption Strategy

✅ Zero Tax Strategy

Every financial year ₹1.25L LTCG gains-க்கு zero tax! ₹1.25L profit realize பண்ணி reinvest → cost basis increase → future tax reduce. Two years = ₹2.5L gains, zero tax!

8. SWP — Regular Income Strategy

Systematic Withdrawal Plan — Monthly fixed amount automatically redeem.

💸 4% Rule

₹1 Crore corpus → ₹4,000/month SWP. Corpus continues growing. Better than FD — only gain portion taxed, principal untouched. Retirement-க்கு ideal!

Setup: AMC portal-ல SWP option → Amount + date → Bank account link → Done!

10. Market Fall-ல Redeem — NEVER!

⚠️ Most Critical Rule

Paper loss = temporary. Redeem பண்ணா permanent! 2020 COVID crash → 2021 recovery 100%+. Exit பண்ணவங்க complete miss. Market fall-ல correct action: Continue SIP + lumpsum add if possible!

11. Common Redemption Mistakes

  • 🚫 Panic sell — Paper loss permanent ஆகும்
  • 🚫 Tax calculate பண்ணாம — Surprise bill வரும்
  • 🚫 Full when partial போதும் — Rest continues compounding!
  • 🚫 Exit load period-ல exit — 1% load waste

13. FAQ

Q: Redeem request-க்கு எத்தனை நாட்கள் வரும்?

✅ Answer

Equity funds: T+2 working days. Debt/Liquid: T+1 working day. 3 PM cut-off — அதுக்கு முன் request same day NAV கிடைக்கும்.

Q: Switch பண்றது tax-ஆ?

Yes! Switch-உம் tax event தான். ஒரு fund-ல இருந்து மற்றொரு fund-க்கு switch = redemption + purchase. LTCG/STCG applicable.
