1. Why Fund Selection Matters

India-ல 1500+ Mutual Funds இருக்கு. Wrong fund choose பண்ணா right market-லயும் wrong results கிடைக்கும்.

⚠️ Wrong Fund Examples

Short-term goal-க்கு Equity fund → Market crash-ல stuck! · Conservative investor-க்கு Small Cap → Panic sell, loss! · High ER fund → 20 years-ல ₹14L extra loss!

✅ Formula

Right Fund = Right Goal + Right Risk + Right Time + Right Cost

💰 Direct Plan Always!

Regular Plan-ஐ விட Direct Plan-ல ER 0.5-1.5% குறைவு. 20 years-ல ₹14L+ difference. Always choose Direct!

6. Shortlisting — 1500+ → 2-3 Funds

  1. 1500+ → 200-300: Goal + Risk + Time → Category filter
  2. 200-300 → 50-100: ER filter, Direct plan only
  3. 50-100 → 15-25: 5yr+ track record, top quartile
  4. 15-25 → 2-3: Manager stability + AMC reputation
  5. Final 2-3: Compare and invest!
🛠️ Tools

Value Research Online (valueresearchonline.com) · Morningstar India · AMC factsheets · MFD-கிட்ட ஒரு conversation-ல done!

7. Funds to Avoid — Red Flags

  • 🚫 NFO — No track record. Avoid new fund offerings.
  • 🚫 Very High ER (2%+) — Math doesn't work long-term.
  • 🚫 Thematic/Sector Rush — IT boom, PSU rally hype funds.
  • 🚫 Frequent Manager Change — 2+ changes in 2 years = avoid.
  • 🚫 Too Small AUM (<₹100 Cr) — Liquidity risk.

10. FAQ

Q: எத்தனை funds வச்சிருக்கணும்?

✅ Answer

3-5 funds போதும். Over-diversification = diluted returns + confusion. Quality > Quantity!

Q: NFO-ல invest பண்ணலாமா?

Generally avoid பண்ணுங்க. Track record இல்லாம performance assess பண்ண முடியாது. Established funds always better choice.
