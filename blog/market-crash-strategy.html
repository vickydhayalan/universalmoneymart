2. Crash Psychology — Why People Panic

Euphoria (peak) → Denial → Fear → Panic (worst exit point) → Despair (actual bottom) → Hope → Regret (missed recovery).

⚠️ The Trap

Panic sellers exit at despair (bottom). Miss full recovery. Re-enter at hope/regret stage = buy high, sell low. Exact opposite of wealth creation!

3. Wrong Reactions — Never Do These!

  • ❌ Panic sell — Paper loss → permanent real loss
  • ❌ SIP stop — Miss cheap units, lower recovery returns
  • ⚠️ Daily news check — Anxiety spiral, emotional decisions
  • ⚠️ Cash hold waiting for bottom — Miss fast recovery, FOMO re-entry at top

4. Right Reactions — Smart Investor Playbook

  • ✓ Switch off news — Quarterly review only
  • ✓ SIP continue always — Crash = cheap units
  • ✓ Lumpsum deploy in stages — Emergency fund intact first
  • ✓ Review fundamentals — Not daily NAV
  • ✓ Step-up SIP if possible — Maximum cost averaging
