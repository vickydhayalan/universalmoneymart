1. Why Goal-Based Investing?

🎯 Core Principle

Every rupee needs a name and purpose.
Random investing: Market-ஐ பாத்து react. No anchor.
Goal-based: '2031-ல house, ₹20L' — crash = temporary setback only!

2. SMART Goal Framework

  • Specific — "₹30L child education fund"
  • Measurable — Exact amount, not "enough money"
  • Achievable — Realistic to current income
  • Relevant — Matches your life stage
  • Time-Bound — "2039 June" not "15 years"

4. Goal 1: Emergency Fund — ALWAYS FIRST!

🚨 Foundation

3-6 months expenses · Liquid Fund (T+1, 6-7%) · Without this, all other goals at risk during personal crisis!

5. Goal 4: Retirement — The Biggest Goal!

👴 25x Rule

Corpus = Annual Expenses × 25
₹1L/month → ₹12L/year → ₹3 Crore corpus needed!

Start at 25: ₹16,000/month · Start at 35: ₹22,000 · Start at 45: ₹55,000+
Time is EVERYTHING. Start today!

6. Goal 5: Financial Independence

FI Number = Annual Expenses × 25. Passive income from corpus covers all expenses. Work becomes optional!

7. Goal Prioritization Order

  1. Emergency Fund — Always first!
  2. ELSS/Tax Saving — Immediate return on investment
  3. Retirement SIP — Time matters most here
  4. Child Education — Start at birth if possible
  5. House Down Payment — After above running
  6. FI/Wealth Creation — As income grows

8. Separate SIP per Goal

💡 Golden Rule

Every goal → separate fund → separate SIP. Never mix! If you mix, goal tracking impossible and emergency goals fail each other.

9. Inflation Adjustment — Real Numbers!

📈 7% Inflation Rule

Today's ₹10L = 10 years-ல ₹19.7L = 15 years-ல ₹27.6L!
Always set inflation-adjusted targets. Education cost: 10%/yr inflation.
Today's education cost × 2.5 = 10-year future cost (approx).
