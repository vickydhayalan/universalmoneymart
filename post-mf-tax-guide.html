Mutual Fund Taxation Simplified: LTCG, STCG, and the Dividend Tax Rule

Mutual Fund la **return** perum bodhu, tax payment **sariyaana maathiri** pannanum. Tax rules **complex** ah irukkum, but **holding period** (evalo kaalam vechchirukkom) and **fund type** (Equity ah, Debt ah) dhaan **tax rate** ah decide pannum. Idhai purinjukka, unga post-tax returns ah maximize panna mudiyum.

The Core Distinction: Equity vs. Non-Equity (Debt) Funds

Taxation la, Mutual Funds irandu categories la piriyum:

Equity Oriented Fund

Fund oda **65% ku mela** Indian **equity** (stocks) la invest pannina, adhaindhaan **Equity Fund** nu solluvanga. Idhukku **low tax rates** irukkum.

Non-Equity / Debt Fund

Fund oda **65% ku kammiya** equity la invest pannina, adhaindhaan **Debt/Non-Equity Fund** nu solluvanga. Idhukku **high tax rates** irukkum.

Equity Taxation: Short-Term (STCG) vs. Long-Term (LTCG)

Holding Period: **12 months** dhaan cut-off.

Holding Period Tax Type Tax Rate
Less than 12 Months Short-Term Capital Gains (STCG) Flat 15%
More than 12 Months Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) 10% (Gains above ₹1 Lakh are taxed)

Tip: Long-term la **₹1 Lakh** varaikum **LTCG free** dhaan. Adhukku mela dhaan **10% tax** apply aagum.

Debt Fund Taxation: The 36-Month Rule and Indexation

Debt Funds ku **tax rules** romba strict da. **36 months** dhaan cut-off.

Holding Period Tax Type Tax Rate
Less than 36 Months Short-Term Capital Gains (STCG) Your Income Tax Slab (Marginal Rate)
More than 36 Months Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) 20% after Indexation (Indexation Benefit)

Indexation Benefit (The Debt Fund Advantage)

Indexation dhaan **inflation effect** ah compensate pannum. Unga **purchase price** ah **inflation rate** ku yetha maathiri increase panni kaattum. Adhunaala **taxable gain** romba kammiya irukkum. Idhu dhaan Debt Funds ah Bank FD vida superior aakkum.

Taxation on Dividends (IDCW)

Ippo **Dividends** (IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) ah Fund la irundhu perum bodhu, adhu **Fully Taxable** dhaan. Adhuvum **unga tax bracket** la dhaan varum.
