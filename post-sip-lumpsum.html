SIP vs Lumpsum: Which is the Best Investment Method?

Neenga market la **invest** panna ready aagum bodhu, **rendu vazhi** dhaan irukku: **SIP** (Systematic Investment Plan) or **Lumpsum** (oru vaati, periya amount). Indha decision dhaan unga **risk** ah, **return** ah decide pannum. Majority investors ku ethu best nu paakalaam.

Understanding the Mechanics

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)

Lumpsum (One-Time Investment)

Lumpsum na, unga kitta irukka **periya amount** ah (E.g., bonus money, property sale) **oru vaati** ah full ah invest panradhu.

The Advantage of SIP (Discipline and Psychology)

Experts SIP ah recommend panradhukku **moondru mukkiyamana kaaranam** irukku:

1. Rupee Cost Averaging (RCA)

Market mela kela pogum bodhu, **RCA** dhaan unga **average unit cost** ah balance pannum. Market low ah irukkum bodhu **neraiya units** vaanguveenga, high ah irukkum bodhu **kammi units** vaanguveenga. Idhu **risk** ah kammi pannum.

2. Eliminating Market Timing Risk

Eppa invest pannanum nu nenaikka vendam. **SIP** ah start panni vitadhukku appuram, **time** dhaan unga **money** ah grow pannum. Timing the market is impossible da.

3. Financial Discipline

SIP **compulsory savings** ah unga life la kondu varum. Idhu unga **long-term goals** ku romba mukkiyam.

When is Lumpsum Ideal?

Lumpsum kooda **nalla option** dhaan, aana indha **rendu condition** la mattum:

  • Clear Market Dip: Market **periya crash** (E.g., 20-30% down) la irundha, adhula **Lumpsum** panna, **high returns** vara chance irukku.
  • Expert Guidance: Unga MFD or Advisor **clear signal** kudutha dhaan, Lumpsum pannanum.

Conclusion: Best Method for Most Investors

SIP dhaan 95% investors ku best method da. Idhu **risk** ah korakkum, **discipline** ah maintain pannum. Lumpsum ah **Market Correction** time la **extra investment** ah (Top-Up) use pannalaam.
