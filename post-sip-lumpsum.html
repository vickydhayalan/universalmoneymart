II. ğŸ’° Option 1: **SIP**-oda Benefits (Consistency is Key)

**SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)** na, **chinna amount**-a **regular-a** (e.g., monthly) invest panradhu. Idhu oru **discipline**-aana savings habit maadhiri.

Monthly Income Irukkuravangalukku Ethuku Best?

Nee **monthly salary** vaangurava-na, **SIP** dhaan **best**. **â‚¹500**-kku kooda **SIP** start panna laam. **Budget**-kku adhu **extra pressure** kodukkadhu.

Main Advantage: Rupee Cost Averaging (RCA)

Idhu dhaan **SIP**-oda **periya advantage** da. **Market high**-a irundhaa, nee **kammi units** vaanguva. **Market low**-a irundhaa, nee **jaasthi units** vaanguva. **Over time**, unga **average purchase cost** kammi aagum. Adhunaala **Risk** automatic-a **kammi** aagum.

III. ğŸ’µ Option 2: **Lumpsum**-oda Power (Time in the Market)

**Lumpsum** na, **periya amount**-a **one-time**-la, **full-a** **Mutual Fund**-la podradhu dhaan.

Bonus Cash Irundhaa Eppdi Use Panradhu?

Unga kitta **Big Bonus** oru **Property Sale** maadhiri **extra cash** irukku-na, adha **Lumpsum**-a invest panna laam. **Time in the Market** principle dhaan romba mukkiyam. **Money early-a** invest aaguradhunaala, **long-term**-la **compound** aaga **jaasthi time** kidaikkum.

IV. âš”ï¸ Head-to-Head Comparison: Risk & Psychology

Indha **2 methods**-la **Risk** and **mindset** eppdi work aagudhu nu paakalaam.

1. Risk Management (Periya Danger)

**Lumpsum**-la **Timing** romba mukkiyam. Nee **Market Peak**-la **full money**-a pottuttaa, adhudan market **down** aana, unga **loss** **romba perusa** irukkum. **SIP**-kku andha **risk** kammi dhaan.

2. Psychological Comfort (Manasuku Thelivu)

**Market crash** aagum bodhu, **SIP** investors **panic** aagave maattanga. Yena avanga **automatically** **cheap rate**-la **jaasthi units** vaanguvanga. **Lumpsum** investors **panic** panna chance irukku. **SIP** dhaan **emotional decisions** edukkaama control pannum.
