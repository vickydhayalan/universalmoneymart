Rupee Cost Averaging (RCA) - Market Timing Illama

Idhu dhaan SIP-oda periya advantage da. Market high-a irundhaa, nee kammi units vaanguva. Market low-a irundhaa, nee jaasthi units vaanguva. Over time, unga average purchase cost kammi aagum.
