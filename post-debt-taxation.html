Debt Fund Taxation: How STCG and LTCG Affect Your Fixed Income Returns

Risk kammiya irukkuradhu dhaan **Debt Funds** and **Fixed Deposits** oda power. Aana, **post-tax return** ku, indha rendukku naduvula **periya difference** irukku. Taxation rules dhaan inga **game-changer** da.

The Core Rule: Short Term vs Long Term (The 36-Month Difference)

Mutual Funds (Debt Funds serththu) la **tax** irandu categories la piriyum, adhu **holding period** ah based pannum:

  • Short-Term Capital Gains (STCG): Investment ah **36 months (3 years) ku munnadi** sell pannina.
  • Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG): Investment ah **36 months ku appuram** sell pannina.

Short-Term Capital Gains (STCG): The Tax Burden

Debt Funds la **STCG** apply aagum bodhu, **ungalaala tax bracket** (Tax Slab) entha rate irukko, adhe rate apply aagum. Example: Neenga **30% bracket** la irundha, 30% tax pay pannanum.

Bank FD Interest Taxation

Bank FD Interest **annual ah** credit aagum, and adhu **fully taxable** dhaan. Adhuvum **STCG** maathiri **unga tax bracket** la dhaan varum. Idhu dhaan **FD** ku **periya drawback** da.

Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) & Indexation: The Debt Fund Advantage

Debt Funds ah **3 varusham** ku mela vechchirundha, adha **LTCG** nu solluvanga. Ippo **Indexation Benefit** dhaan **game-changer**:

  • Indexation: Idhu **inflation effect** ah **kammi** pannum. Unga **purchase cost** ah **inflate** panni kaattum, adhunaala **taxable gain** ah korakkum.
  • Final Rate: LTCG tax rate is 20% after indexation. Idhu dhaan **effective tax rate** ah **romba kammi** ah aakkum.

Debt Fund vs Bank FD: Why Taxation Makes Debt Funds Superior

Low-risk investment ku, **post-tax return** la Debt Funds dhaan win aagum:

Feature Debt Mutual Fund Bank Fixed Deposit (FD)
Annual Income Tax Nil (Only at redemption) Fully Taxable (Every year)
Tax Rate (3+ Years) 20% After Indexation Your Tax Slab (Up to 30%)
Liquidity Easy (T+1/T+2 days) Break penalty (Fixed Lock-in)
