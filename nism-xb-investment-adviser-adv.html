I. üéØ Introduction: Why NISM X-B is the Final Step

NISM X-A dhaan **basic**. Aana **IA Level 2 (X-B)** dhaan unga **advisory skills**-a **expert level**-kku kondu pogum. Indha exam-a clear panna, nee **SEBI**-oda **IA Regulations**-kku **full-a qualify** aaga laam.

Indha exam-oda focus **complex financial products**, **client behavior**, and **advanced retirement strategies**-la dhaan jaasthi irukkum.

II. üìú Exam Structure & Complexity

Series X-B exam **IA Level 1**-a vida **romba detailed**-a irukkum.

Series X-B Exam Pattern and Complexity

  • Total Questions: 100 MCQs
  • Passing Mark: 60% (60 marks) üî¥
  • Negative Marking: Yes, 0.25% irukkum.

Core Difference: **X-A**-la **theory** jaasthi. **X-B**-la **advanced calculations** and **case study** based questions jaasthi irukkum.

üí° Study Tip

NISM X-B-kku **Client Profiling** (Risk and Goal) pathi **romba practical-a** padikkanum. **Ethical** questions romba tricky-a irukkum.

III. üß† Core Skill 1: **Behavioral Finance** (Client Mindset)

Expert IA-kku **client**-oda **mindset** pathi puriyum. Adhu dhaan **Behavioral Finance**.

Understanding Client Emotional Mistakes

Exam-la **investors** **bayam** (fear) illa **aasai** (greed)-aala **eppdi thappu** pannuvanga nu padikkanum.

  • Cognitive Biases: **Thinking Mistakes** (e.g., **Overconfidence**-aala stock-a hold panradhu).
  • Emotional Biases: **Feeling Mistakes** (e.g., **Panic**-aala sell panradhu).

IA Level 2-kku, indha **biases**-a identify panni, **correct advice** kudukka theriyanum.

IV. üìä Core Skill 2: **Advanced Retirement** and Tax Planning

Retirement Planning pathi **X-B**-la **romba detailed**-a irukkum.

Cash Flow Strategy (SWP)

Retirement time-la **inflation**-a handle panna, **SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)** maadhiri **cash flow strategies** pathi padikkanum. **Pension Fund Schemes** and **Advanced Tax Structures** pathi knowledge venum.

V. üí° Core Skill 3: **Complex Products**

High Net Worth Individuals (HNI)-kku advice thara, indha knowledge venum.

REITs, InvITs, and Alternative Funds (AIFs)

  • REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts): **Real Estate**-la **investment** panna oru **easy way**.
  • InvITs (Infrastructure Investment Trusts): **Roads**, **Power plants** maadhiri **infrastructure**-la **invest** panradhu.
  • AIFs (Alternative Investment Funds): **Complex funds** (Venture Capital, Hedge Funds) pathi padikkanum.
