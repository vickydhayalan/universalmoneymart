II. ğŸ’¡ What is a Mutual Fund? (The Simple Buffet Analogy)

**Simple-a** sollanum-na, oru **Hotel Buffet** maadhiri dhaan da.

Buffet Concept (Analogy)

Nee **single-a** oru **periya dish** (single stock) **vaangama**, **pala dish** (different stocks, bonds, gold) irukkura oru **Buffet**-kku **chinna amount** pay panra. **Niraiya per** (investors) **chinna amount** serthu, **periya pool** of money create pannuvanga.

Andha **periya money pool**-a oru **Expert Chef** (Fund Manager) dhaan **ethu nalla dish** nu therinju **invest** pannuvanga. Adhu dhaan **Mutual Fund**!

III. ğŸ“Š Key Concepts: NAV and Fund Manager

Mutual Fund-a purinjikka, **rendu vaarththaigal** romba mukkiyam:

1. NAV (Net Asset Value)

**NAV** na oru **Mutual Fund**-oda **unit price** da. Nee **Grocery shop**-la oru **biscuit packet**-a vaangura maadhiri, nee **MF**-la **units** vaanguva. Oru oru **unit**-kku enna **price** nu kaattradhu dhaan **NAV**.

2. Fund Manager

Ivar dhaan **Expert Chef**! **Fund Manager** dhaan unga **money**-a **research** panni, **ethu best stocks** nu select panni **invest** pannuvanga. Ungalukku **time** illai-na, ivanga **knowledge**-a use panna laam.
