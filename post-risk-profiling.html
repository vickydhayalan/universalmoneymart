Risk Profiling: How to Determine Your True Risk Appetite and Portfolio Allocation

Investing la **endha fund** ah choose panra dhu nu theriyanumna, **Risk Profiling** dhaan first step. Idhu **scientific method** ah use panni, unga **money ah enga podanum** nu sollum. Irandu mukkiyamana factors irukku: Risk Tolerance and Risk Capacity.

Risk Tolerance vs. Risk Capacity (Psychology vs. Financial Reality)

Risk Tolerance na, **psychologically** neenga evalo loss ah **handle panna ready** ah irukkeenga nu artham. **Risk Capacity** na, **financially** neenga evalo loss ah **handle panna mudiyum** nu artham. Rendu-me different da.

The 4 Key Factors Determining Your Risk Profile

Neenga **SIP** ah **aggressive** ah pannanuma illa **conservative** ah pannanuma nu indha factors dhaan decide pannum:

1. Time Horizon (The Long-Term View)

Unga **Goal** ku innum **neraiya time** (E.g., 10+ years for Retirement) irundha, neenga **higher risk** (Equity) edukkalam. **Goal pakkathula** (1-3 years) irundha, **low risk** (Debt, FD) ku ponganum.

2. Financial Capacity (The Money Shield)

Unga **Emergency Fund** strong ah irukkaa? **Income** stability ah irukkaa? **Net worth** nalla irundha, **capacity** athigam, so **risk** edukkalam.

3. Current Age and Dependencies

Young investors (20s-30s) ku, **risk capacity** romba athigam. Yenendraal, **neraiya time** irukku loss ah cover panna. Retirement ku pakkathula irundha, **low risk** ku poga vendum.

4. Psychological Comfort (Sleep Factor)

Market crash aagum bodhu, **ungalaala nimmadhiya thunga mudiyuma**? Mudiyala na, unga **risk tolerance** kammi nu artham. Adhai **accept** panni, **low risk funds** la invest pannunga.

Mapping Risk to Products (Allocation Strategy)

Unga risk profile ku yetha maathiri **Assets** ah **eppadi divide** pannanum nu indha table sollum:

Risk Profile Asset Allocation (MF) Suitable Products
Conservative (Low Risk) 80% Debt / 20% Equity Liquid Funds, Fixed Deposits, Short Duration Debt Funds
Moderate 40% Debt / 60% Equity Hybrid Funds, Large Cap Equity Funds
Aggressive (High Risk) 10% Debt / 90% Equity Small Cap, Mid Cap, and Sectoral Equity Funds
