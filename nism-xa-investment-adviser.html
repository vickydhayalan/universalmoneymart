MFD (Distributor) dhaan ARN vechchu, Mutual Fund-a sell panna **permission** irukkum. Aana Investment Adviser (IA) dhaan fees vaangi **direct financial advice** kudukka **legal-a** **authorize** aaguranga.

NISM X-A exam dhaan **IA** aaga **first mandatory step**. Indha exam-oda focus **Financial Planning**-la dhaan jaasthi irukkum.

III. üìä Core Skill 1: **Client Profiling** and Goal Setting

IA-oda first job dhaan client-a **nalla purinjikradhu**.

1. KYC vs Risk Profiling

  • KYC (Know Your Client): Basic Identity (Name, Address, PAN) pathi dhaan.
  • Risk Profiling: Risk Tolerance (Emotionally ethana loss-a handle panna mudiyum?) and Risk Capacity (Financially ethana loss-a handle panna mudiyum?) pathi **deep analysis** panradhu.

2. Defining Financial Goals

Exam-la **Goal Setting** pathi romba questions varum. Goals-a Time Horizon-a vachchu divide pannanum:

Goal Categories and Investment

  • Short-Term Goals (1-3 years): Car Down Payment, Vacation. (Invest in **Debt Funds**).
  • Medium-Term Goals (3-7 years): House Down Payment, Child's Education Start. (Invest in **Hybrid Funds**).
  • Long-Term Goals (10+ years): Retirement. (Invest in **Equity/Growth Funds**).

IV. üí° Core Skill 2: **Asset Allocation** and Planning

Client-oda profiling mudinjathum, **money-a eppdi divide** pannanum nu **plan** pannanum.

Strategic Asset Allocation

Strategic Allocation dhaan IA-oda main velai. Client-oda age, income, and time horizon-a vachchu **Equity** and **Debt**-kku **eppdi money-a divide** pannanum nu sollitharum.
