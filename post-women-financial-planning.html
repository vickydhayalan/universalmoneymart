Financial Planning for Women: Addressing Unique Investment Challenges

Women ku **investment** la **unique challenges** irukku da. Adhu **Longevity Risk** and **Career Gaps**. Ippadiyana situations ah **sariyaana maathiri** plan panni dhaan **financial independence** achieve panna mudiyum.

The Core Challenge: Longevity and Career Gaps

Women **men vida** **long time** dhaan **uyiroda** iruppanga. Adhunaala, **retirement corpus** **romba perusa** thevai aagum. Plus, **career breaks** (childcare, family care) varum bodhu, **savings** and **compounding** la **loss** varum.

Challenge 1: The Longevity Factor

Retirement ku appuram **25-30 years** ku **income** venum. Idhukku **aggresive growth** romba mukkiyam.

Challenge 2: Career Gaps and Compounding Loss

Career la **break** varum bodhu, **monthly SIP** ah **stop** panna vendiyadhu irukkum. Andha time la **compounding** benefit ah miss pannuvanga.

The Strategy: Aggressive Goal Setting and Automation

Indha **unique challenges** ah meet panna, indha **moondru strategies** ah follow pannanum:

  • Early Start & Aggressive Allocation: 20s laye **SIP** start pannanum. **High Equity** allocation (80%+) vechchukonga.
  • Separate Protection: **Term Insurance** and **Health Insurance** **separate ah** vechchukonga. Family health cover romba mukkiyam.
  • Automation & Discipline: **Step-Up SIP** use panni, **annual increase** ah automate pannunga. **Emergency Fund** ah **liquid** ah vechchukonga.

Prioritizing Financial Independence

Women ku **Financial Independence** dhaan **highest priority** da. Husband's income ah mattum nambi irukka koodadhu. **Self-income** la **separate investment** ah maintain pannanum.
