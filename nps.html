Statutory Warning: NPS is a market-linked product. Past performance of fund managers does not guarantee future returns.

Understanding Tier I and Tier II Accounts

Tier I Account (Mandatory)

Primary retirement account with strict lock-in until age 60. This is the account eligible for all major tax deductions (80C and 80CCD(1B)).

Tier II Account (Voluntary)

Works like a voluntary savings account. No tax benefits, but allows withdrawal of corpus anytime. Ideal for those who have exhausted other options.

Fund Management

Choose between active (E-Equity, C-Corporate Debt, G-Government Debt) or auto choice fund allocation based on your age and risk profile.
