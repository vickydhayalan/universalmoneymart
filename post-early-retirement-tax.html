Early Retirement Strategy: Using SWP and Capital Gains Tax Rules to Retire Before Age 60

Early Retirement (E.g., 50s la retirement) oru **periya goal** da. Aana, **government retirement age** (60) ku munnadi **money** edutha, **tax rules** and **liquidity** ah **sariyaana maathiri** manage pannanum. SWP and **Capital Gains Tax** dhaan unga **main tools** da.

The Critical Challenge: Sustaining Income Without Penalty

Retirement ku munnadi **EPF/NPS** maathiri **locked funds** ah edukkum bodhu, **penalty** illana **heavy tax** apply aagum. Adhaala, **Mutual Funds** la irundhu **Tax-Efficient** ah money edukkum **strategy** venum.

The SWP Solution: Creating a Tax-Efficient Paycheck

SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) dhaan **Early Retirement** ku **best income strategy** da. Idhukku **moondru periya benefits** irukku:

1. Tax Efficiency Over Income Tax

Nee **SWP** la money edukkum bodhu, **LTCG (Long-Term Capital Gains)** rule apply aagum. Idhu **Income Tax** (30% bracket) vida **romba kammi** ah irukkum (10% ku mela).

2. Corpus Preservation

SWP la, unga money **market la invest** ah irukkum. Adhu **grow** aagum. Adhunaala, unga **corpus** ah **romba naal** sustain panna mudiyum.

Capital Gains Strategy for Pre-60 Retirement

SWP ku **Tax** ah **minimize** panna, indha **strategy** ah follow pannunga:

  • Utilize ₹1 Lakh LTCG Exemption: Oru varusham la **₹1 Lakh** varaikkum **LTCG free** dhaan. Namma **SWP** ah **sariyaana maathiri** set panna, indha benefit ah use panna mudiyum.
  • Maintain Long-Term Status: **Withdrawal** ku munnadi **12 months** ku **Equity Funds** ah **hold** pannirukkanu confirm pannunga. Appo dhaan **LTCG (10%)** apply aagum, **STCG (15%)** illa.

Planning the Transition

Retirement ku **5 varusham** munnadi, **Equity** funds ah **Debt/Liquid Funds** ku **STP** use panni **shift** pannanum. Adhu dhaan **market risk** ah kammi pannum.
