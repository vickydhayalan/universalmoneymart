The 3 Layers of Financial Security: Emergency Fund, Insurance, then Investing

Oru strong building build panna, base dhaan mukkiyam. Financial house kooda adhe maathiri dhaan. Neraiya peru **direct ah** **SIP** la money poduvanga, aana **base** weak ah irukkum. Indha **3 layers** ah correct ah follow pannanum.

Layer 1: The Safety Net (Emergency Fund)

What is it?

Unga income stop aanaalum (e.g., job loss, medical break), **minimum 6 months** ku **unga expenses** ah meet panna thevaiyana money dhaan Emergency Fund.

Where to keep it? Bank FD, Liquid Mutual Funds maathiri **easy ah edukka koodiya** edathula vechchukonga. Idhu **risk** edukka koodadhu.

Why it matters: Emergency Fund illa na, **financial shock** varum bodhu, neenga **SIPs** ah illa **FDs** ah break panna vendiyadhu irukkum. Adhu dhaan **periya loss**.

Layer 2: Risk Protection (Term & Health Insurance)

What is it?

Idhu **periya financial risk** ah **transfer** pannum. Medical emergencies ku **Health Insurance** (Health Insurance link) and unga **family** ah protect panna **Term Insurance** (Term Insurance link) romba mukkiyam.

Why it matters: Oru major illness vandha, **insurance illa na**, unga **ellam savings** ah eduthu **expenses** ah meet panna vendiyadhu irukkum. Adhu dhaan **financial future** ah destroy pannum.

Layer 3: Wealth Creation (SIP & Investing)

What is it?

Ippo **layers 1 and 2 strong** ah irukku. Ippo dhaan neenga **SIP** use panni **Equity Mutual Funds** la money podanum. Idhudhaan **long-term wealth** create panna correct time.

The Priority: Always invest **Term Insurance** and **SIP** ku appuram dhaan, remaining income ah use pannanum.

Final Word: Namma MFD ah, indha **moondru layers** ah **correct ah plan** panna dhaan help panrom. Epozhum **foundation** ah strong ah vechchukonga.
