**Mutual Funds**-la invest panna varum bodhu, **Large Cap, Mid Cap, Small Cap** nu niraiya **complicated** vaarththaigal varum. Idhellam **company size**-a dhaan da kaattudhu.

Unga **money** **ethana risk**-kku exposed aagum nu therinjikka, indha **size difference**-a purinjikradhu **romba mukkiyam**. Unga **risk appetite**-kku ethu match aagum nu paakalaam.

II. ğŸ‘‘ Large Cap: The Kings of the Market

**Large Cap** funds **periya companies** (e.g., Reliance, TCS, HDFC Bank) dhaan invest pannuvanga. Indha companies dhaan market-la **Top 100**-la irukkum.

High Safety and Stable Returns

Indha companies **market-la established** aagi, **romba periya network** irukkuradhunaala:

  • **Risk:** **Low Risk** dhaan. Market down aanalum, adha **handle** panna **periya capacity** irukkum.
  • **Return:** **Returns** konjam **slow**-a irukkum (Stable Growth). Aana **kandippa** steady-a **compound** aagum.

III. ğŸ¹ Mid Cap: The Growth Warriors

**Mid Cap** funds **101st company**-la irundhu **250th company** varaià®•à¯à®•à¯à®®à¯ irukkura **medium-sized companies** dhaan focus pannuvanga. Indha companies **fast-a grow** aagi, **Large Cap** aaga try pannuvanga.

Balance of Risk and High Growth

**Mid Cap** funds **risky**-aanaalum, **returns** romba **attractive**-a irukkum.

  • **Risk:** **Medium Risk**. **Volatility** (price change) **Large Cap**-a vida jaasthi irukkum.
  • **Return:** **Fast growth**-kku **chance** irukku. **Long-term**-kku **Mid Cap** romba nalla return kodukkum.

âœ… Key Takeaways (Mid Cap Caution)

  • **Mid Cap** **risk**-um **return**-um oru **balance**-la irukkum.
  • **Beginners**, **SIP**-a **50:50** (Large Cap: Mid Cap) maadhiri mix panna laam.

IV. ğŸ¦ Small Cap: The High-Risk, High-Reward Players

**Small Cap** funds **251st company**-kku mela irukkura **chinna companies** dhaan da. Indha companies **new ideas** and **innovative** business-la irukkum.

Highest Risk, Maximum Returns Potential

**Small Cap**-la **periya return** vara **chance** irukkura maadhiri, **periya loss** vara chance-um irukku.

  • **Risk:** **Highest Risk**. Market **volatility** romba jaasthi irukkum. **Recession** varum bodhu, indha funds dhaan **fast-a drop** aagum.
  • **Return:** **Romba periya returns** (multibagger) vara chance irukku. Aana adhu **long-term** (10+ years) **patience**-oda irundhaa dhaan kidaikkum.

V. ğŸ“Š Conclusion: Portfolio-la Eppdi Mix Pannanum?

**Diversify** panradhu dhaan **best rule** da. Unga **risk capacity**-kku eththa maadhiri **mix** pannanum.

  • **Low Risk Takers (Beginners):** **70% Large Cap, 30% Mid Cap**. Small Cap vendam.
  • **Medium Risk Takers (Young Professionals):** **50% Large Cap, 40% Mid Cap, 10% Small Cap**.
  • **High Risk Takers:** Small Cap-kku konjam jaasthi **allocation** kudukka laam.
