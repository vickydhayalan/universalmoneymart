Large Cap funds periya companies (e.g., Reliance, TCS, HDFC Bank) dhaan invest pannuvanga. Indha companies dhaan SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India) list-la Top 100-la irukkum.

Small Cap funds SEBI list-la 251st company-kku mela irukkura chinna companies dhaan da. Indha companies new ideas and innovative business-la irukkum.
