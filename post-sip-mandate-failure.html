SIP Payment Failure: Understanding NACH Mandates and Ensuring Consistency

SIP dhaan **Discipline** ku mukkiyam. Aana, **SIP** start panna appuram, **payment failure** aana, adhu unga **long-term return** ah damage pannum. NACH Mandate and **ECS** system dhaan indha automatic payment ku help pannum.

What is a NACH Mandate? (The Automatic Debit System)

NACH (National Automated Clearing House) dhaan **RBI** use panra system. Idhu **Fund House** ah unga **bank account** la irundhu **sariyaana time** la **SIP amount** ah **debit** panna **permission** kudukkum.

  • **One-Time Setup:** Mandate ah **oru vaati** dhaan setup pannanum (usually 15 days la complete aagum).
  • **Limit:** Neenga **maximum withdrawal limit** ah fix panna mudiyum.

Why SIPs Fail (Common Causes)

**Payment failure** ku **moondru main reasons** irukku:

1. Insufficient Funds (The Primary Cause)

Debit aagum bodhu, unga **bank balance** la **SIP amount** illa na, **payment fail** aagum. Bank **penalty** kooda podalaam.

2. Expired or Faulty Mandate

Bank **merge** aana appuram illa **account details** change aana, **old mandate** **work aagaadhu**. Adhunaala **re-registration** panna vendum.

The Consequences of a Failed SIP

SIP fail aana, adhu **rendu periya loss** ah kudukkum:

  • **Missed Compounding:** Andha month ku **units** vaanga mudiyadhu. Compounding benefit ah miss pannuveenga.
  • **Bank Penalty:** Unga bank, **transaction failure** ku **penalty charges** poduvanga.

Strategy: Troubleshooting and Fixing Your Mandate

SIP consistency dhaan mukkiyam. Failure aana, udane indha steps ah follow pannunga:

Solution: **Bank balance** ah **Debit Date** ku munnadi check pannunga. Failure aana, **udane** money ah bank la pottu, **MFD** kitta **missed SIP** ah **lumpsum** ah panna sollu. Adhu dhaan **loss** ah kammi pannum.
