SEBI's Role in Investor Protection: The Regulatory Framework for Mutual Funds

Share Market la **stocks** ah **live price** la vaanguvom, aana **Mutual Funds** ku **NAV** (Net Asset Value) dhaan use aagum. **Price** and **NAV** ku naduvula irukka **main difference** ah purinjukka romba mukkiyam. Idhu dhaan neenga entha rate ku vaangureenga nu sollum.

Who is SEBI and Why Are They Important? (The Watchdog)

SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India) dhaan **Indian Capital Market** oda **regulatory body** da. SEBI dhaan **investors** ah **protect** panni, **fair and transparent** market ah maintain pannum.

**SEBI** dhaan **Trustee, AMC, and Custodian** maathiri **moondru main entities** ah **regulate** pannum. Adhaala, **AMC** ku **thappa panna** **chance illa** da.

SEBI's Key Mandates for AMCs (Investor Safety Rules)

SEBI **Mutual Fund** industry ku **romba strict rules** kuduthirukku. Idhudhaan **investor money** ah **safe** ah vechchukka help pannum:

  • Asset Segregation: AMC oda **own money** and **investor money** ah **separate** ah vechchukonum.
  • Trustee Oversight: **Trustee Board** la **independent members** dhaan irukkanum.
  • NAV Calculation Rule: **NAV** ah **sariyaana maathiri** dhaan **calculate** pannanum.

Ensuring Transparency and Disclosure

SEBI **Transparency** ku romba mukkiyam kudukkum. **Ellam information** ah **investors** ku **clear ah** sollanum.

  • Factsheet Disclosure: Fund oda **performance, risk ratios, and expenses** ah **monthly** ah **mandatorily** release pannanum.
  • Expense Ratio Limit: **Expense Ratio** ku **SEBI** dhaan **maximum limit** fix pannum. Adhukku mela charge panna koodadhu.

Investor Grievance and Resolution

Investors ku **ethana problem** irundhaalum, **SEBI** dhaan **redressal mechanism** (SCORES platform) ah kudukkum. Idhu **fairness** ah maintain pannum.

Invest with Confidence in a Regulated Market!

Unga money **regulated structure** la irukku. Namma MFD ah, unga **SEBI compliance** ku help pannuvom.
