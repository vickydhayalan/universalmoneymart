Year-Round Tax Planning: Beyond the March Deadline

Tax planning la **romba periya thappu**, **March month** varum varai **wait** panradhu dhaan. Apdi panna, neenga **hasty decisions** edukkanum, andha decisions **investment return** ah **damage** pannum. Proactive Tax Planning dhaan **smart way**.

Mistake #1: The March Rush (Why Procrastination Costs Money)

March la tax save panna try pannum bodhu, neenga **Lumpsum** ah periya amount ah ELSS la poduveenga. Market peak la irundhaalum, **loss** ah avoid panna mudiyadhu. Idhu dhaan **panic investing**.

Solution: Use **SIPs in ELSS** starting from **April**. Idhu **Rupee Cost Averaging** benefit ah kudukkum, and March la **extra burden** irukkaadhu.

Maximizing the ₹2 Lakh Benefit (The Three Tax Pillars)

Income Tax Act la, neenga **₹2 Lakhs** varaikkum deduction claim panna mudiyum. Idhudhaan **ellam tax benefits** ah use panra strategy:

Pillar 1: Section 80C (₹1.5 Lakhs Limit)

Idhu dhaan **primary limit**. Idhula **ELSS Mutual Funds**, **PPF**, **Term Insurance**, **Life Insurance** premium maathiri ellam varum. Namma **ELSS SIP** dhaan recommend panrom for **wealth creation**.

Pillar 2: Section 80CCD(1B) (Extra ₹50,000)

Idhu dhaan **exclusive benefit** for **NPS (National Pension System)**. Neenga **80C** limit ah **full ah** use pannitaalum, **NPS** la **extra ₹50,000** invest panna, **total deduction** ah **₹2 Lakhs** ku mela kondu poga mudiyum. Idhu romba **valuable** da.

Pillar 3: Section 80D (Health Insurance)

Idhu kooda romba mukkiyam. **Health Insurance premium** (Term and Health) ku **extra deduction** kidaikkum (₹25,000 to ₹50,000). Idhu **protection** and **tax saving** ah serththu kudukkum.

The April to March Strategy (Proactive Plan)

Unga **tax saving** ah **month by month** divide pannanum:

  • **April - June:** **Insurance** (Term & Health) premium ah pay pannunga.
  • **April - December:** **ELSS SIP** ah start pannunga. Idhu ₹1.5 Lakhs ku contribution kudukkum.
  • **January:** **Final review** panni, **pending balance** irundha, **NPS** la **lumpsum** ah panni **₹50,000** deduction ah claim pannunga.
