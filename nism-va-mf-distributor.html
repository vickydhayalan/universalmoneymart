NAV Calculation Example

NAV na oru fund-oda **Net Asset Value** dhaan.

Simple NAV Calculation

Formula:

NAV = (Total Assets - Total Liabilities) / Total Units Outstanding

  • Total Assets: ‚Çπ200 Crores
  • Total Liabilities (Expenses): ‚Çπ2 Crores
  • Units Outstanding: 10 Crores Units

Calculation: (200 - 2) / 10 = **‚Çπ19.80**

Unga NAV is ‚Çπ19.80. Indha mathiri sums-a nalla practice pannunga.
