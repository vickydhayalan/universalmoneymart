The Blueprint to Wealth: Setting & Prioritizing Financial Goals

Investing la **return** ku munnadi, **Goal** dhaan romba mukkiyam. Goal illa na, unga investments-ku **direction** illa da. Ippo, **SMART Goal** setting method use panni, unga goals ah **eppadi define** pannanum nu paakalaam.

Why Goals are More Important Than Returns

Oru fund **15% return** kuduthaalum, unga **money** ah **4 years** la edukkum bodhu, **loss** vara chance irukku. Goal setting dhaan unga **risk capacity** ah fix pannum:

  • Time Horizon: Short-term goals (1-3 years) ku **low-risk products** (FD, Debt Funds) dhaan venum. Long-term (7+ years) ku **Equity** venum.
  • Discipline Anchor: Market down aanaalum, **goal** ah paaththu **panic selling** ah avoid panna mudiyum.

The Prioritization Pyramid (Need vs. Want)

Unga goals ah **moondru pyramid layers** la pirichu, **layer 1** ah dhaan **first** fill pannanum:

Layer 1: Financial Security (The Foundation)

Idhudhaan **base**. Emergency Fund (6-9 months expense) and **Life/Health Insurance** (Term Insurance) ku dhaan **first priority** kudukkanum. Idhu illa na, **Layer 3** break aagum.

Layer 2: Lifestyle Goals (Medium Term)

Car down payment, **international vacation** maathiri goals. Idhukku **3 - 7 years** time irukkuradhunaala, **Hybrid Funds** maathiri **balanced products** ah use pannalaam.

Layer 3: Legacy Goals (Long Term)

Retirement Corpus and **Children's Education/Marriage**. Idhudhaan **highest priority** kudukka vendiya goals. Idhukku **pure Equity (SIP)** dhaan mukkiyam.

Time Horizon and Product Allocation

Unga goals ah **sariyaana maathiri map** panna, indha table ah paarrunga:

Goal Horizon Example Goal Recommended Product
Short Term (1-3 years) Emergency Fund, Down Payment Liquid Funds, Fixed Deposits
Medium Term (3-7 years) Car Upgrade, Large Vacation Hybrid Funds, Arbitrage Funds
Long Term (7+ years) Retirement, Child's Education Equity Mutual Funds (SIP)
