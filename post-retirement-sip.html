Retirement Goal SIP Calculation: Are You Saving Enough for Your Future?

Retirement Planning is not about how much you earn today, but about predicting **how much you will need** when your income stops. Idhukku, namma **inflation** and **investment returns** ah serththu calculation pannanum. Don't guess; calculate!

The Crucial First Step: Calculating Your Future Expense

Unga retirement time la, unga current monthly expense **inflation** aala **romba perusa** maarirukkum. Inflation-ai ignore panna koodadhu.

Formula for Future Expense

Namma **Future Value (FV) formula** use panni, unga **40,000 monthly expense** (for example) 30 varusham kalichchu **evalo aagum** nu calculate pannanum. (Calculator la irukka **Inflation Rate** use pannunga).

FV (Future Expense) = Current Expense × (1 + Inflation Rate)Years to Retirement

If you have 30 years left and the average inflation is 6%, your current ₹40,000 monthly expense will be over ₹2,29,740 per month! This huge number is your new target.

The Ultimate Goal: Calculating the Required Corpus

Retirement ku appuram, unga money **mudiyave koodadhu** (End of Corpus is dangerous). Adhukku namma **Corpus** ah intha maathiri plan pannanum: Withdrawal ku appuram kooda, **remaining money** market la **sariyaana maathiri growth** aaganum.

The Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR) Method

Namma **safe withdrawal rate** use pannanum. SWR = Expected Investment Return minus Inflation Rate. Adhukku appuram, Future Expense ah SWR aala divide pannanum.

Required Corpus = (Future Annual Expense) ÷ (Return Rate - Inflation Rate)

Example: If your Return is 12% and Inflation is 6%, SWR is 6%. If your future expense is ₹30 Lakhs annually, your required corpus is **₹5 Crores**.

The Action Plan: Finding the Monthly SIP Required Today

Ippo unga final target **(Required Corpus)** therinjiduchi. Adhai achieve panna, ippo neenga **monthly SIP** la **evalo amount** panna vendum nu paakkanum. Idhukku **Future Value of Annuity** formula ah reverse use pannanum.

  • Start Today: Retirement planning la **time dhaan** romba mukkiyam. Time delay aagum bodhu, **required SIP amount** romba **increase** aagum.
  • Adjust Annually: Year ku oru vaati **Step Up SIP** use panni, unga SIP amount ah increase pannunga.
