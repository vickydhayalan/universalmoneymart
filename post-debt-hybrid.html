**Debt Funds**-la **risk** **romba kammi** dhaan. Unga money-a **fixed income** instruments-la dhaan invest pannuvanga.

What are Fixed Income Instruments?

Unga money-a **Government**-oda **Bonds**, **Corporate Bonds**, and **Treasury Bills** maadhiri **safe instruments**-la dhaan poduvanga. Idhula **interest** dhaan varum, **market fluctuation**-a pathi **periya tension** venam.

Ideal for Short-Term Goals

**Emergency Fund** vechirukkura idathula **Liquid Funds** (Debt Fund-oda type) use panna laam. **1-3 years**-kku **short-term goal** (e.g., Car down payment) irundhaa, **Debt Funds** romba useful.

III. ğŸ¤ Hybrid Funds: The Balance Masters

**Hybrid Funds** oru **smart choice** da. Idhu **Equity** and **Debt**-oda **perfect mix** dhaan.

How the Balance Works

Idhula **Growth**-kku **Equity** (Stocks)-um, **Safety**-kku **Debt**-um serthu **mix** panniyiruppanga. **Market** **romba high** aagum bodhu, **Fund Manager** **Equity**-a kammi panni **Debt**-la jaasthi poduvanga. **Market low** aagum bodhu, **Equity**-a jaasthi vaanguvanga.

Balanced Advantage (The Best of Both)

**Hybrid Funds** dhaan **Moderate Risk** investors-kku best. Adhu **market volatility**-a **easy-a handle** panni, **stable growth** tharum. **Debt Funds**-a vida **jaasthi return** kidaikkum.
