Top 5 Common Mistakes While Investing in Mutual Funds

Successful investing is more about avoiding big mistakes than finding the 'next big fund.' Most investors lose money not because of poor funds, but because of poor decisions made during stress. Idhudhaan Behavioral Finance nu solluvanga. Indha 5 mistakes ah kandippa avoid pannanum.

Mistake #1: Stopping SIPs During Market Crashes

Idhudhaan **Number one** mistake da. Market down aagum bodhu, **panic** aagi **SIPs ah stop** panradhu. Market low price la irukkum bodhu dhaan, neenga **neraiya units** ah vaanga mudiyum. SIP stop panna, neenga **Rupee Cost Averaging** benefit ah miss panniduveenga.

Solution: Keep your SIP running! Market falls are sales; they are not financial emergencies.

Mistake #2: Chasing Past Returns (The FOMO Trap)

Oru fund **last year** romba **nalla return** koduthadhu nu paathuttu, adhula **money** podradhu. Idhudhaan **FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)** trap. Mutual fund la **Past Returns** future la **guarantee** illa da.

Solution: Choose funds based on your goal, risk profile, and the fund manager's long-term process, not just the last year's performance.

Mistake #3: Market Timing (Buying Low, Selling High)

Market **eppa mela pogum** illana **eppa thiruppi kela varum** nu **namma yaarum predict panna mudiyadhu**. Neenga market timing panna try panna, mostly **thappa dhaan** mudiyum. Idhudhaan professional advisors kooda panna koodaadhu nu solluvanga.

Solution: Stick to your planned **SIP dates**. Time in the market is more important than timing the market.

Mistake #4: Mixing Investment and Insurance

Traditional plans (E.g., ULIPs, Endowment policies) la, **protection** (insurance) and **investment** (returns) **rendume serndhu** irukkum. Idhula **hidden charges** romba athigam, and **returns** romba kammiya irukkum. Namma **Term Insurance** la paarthom, **pure protection** ah mattum edukkanum.

Solution: **Buy Term, Invest Remainder.** Term Insurance ah protection ku mattum use pannunga, and Mutual Funds ah investment ku use pannunga.

Mistake #5: Investing Without a Financial Goal

Neenga **ethukku invest** panreenga nu theriyama, friends சொன்னாங்க nu money podradhu. Goal illa na, market down aagum bodhu, **panic** aagi **eduthuduveenga**. Goal (Retirement, Child's Education) irundha, adhu dhaan unga **discipline** ku anchor ah irukkum.

Solution: Use the **Retirement Planner** or **SIP Calculator** to map your investments to a specific date and amount.
