NAV vs. Price: Understanding How Mutual Fund Units Are Bought and Sold

Share Market la **stocks** ah **live price** la vaanguvom, aana **Mutual Funds** ku **NAV** (Net Asset Value) dhaan use aagum. **Price** and **NAV** ku naduvula irukka **main difference** ah purinjukka romba mukkiyam. Idhu dhaan neenga entha rate ku vaangureenga nu sollum.

What is NAV (Net Asset Value)?

NAV na, oru **Mutual Fund** oda **oru unit** oda value dhaan. Idhu **oru naal la oru vaati** dhaan calculate aagum.

The Simple Formula

NAV = (Total Assets - Total Liabilities) / Total Number of Outstanding Units

Market close aana appuram, **ellam stocks and bonds** oda value ah serththu, **ellam expenses** ah korachchu, adhu ku appuram dhaan **final NAV** ah announce pannuvanga.

The Difference from Stock Pricing (No Real-Time Trading)

Stocks ah **exchange** la **live** ah trade pannuvom. Price **every second** change aagum. Aana, Mutual Funds ah **exchange** la trade panna maattom. Adhaala:

  • No Live Price: Mutual Fund ku **live price** illa. Neenga order kudukkum bodhu, **entha NAV** ku kidaikkum nu appove solla mudiyadhu.
  • End-of-Day Pricing: Neenga **Monday** order kudutha, adhu **Monday evening** oda NAV ku dhaan process aagum.

The Time Stamp Rule: Cut-Off Timing

Unga order ku **entha naal oda NAV** kidaikkum nu **cut-off time** dhaan decide pannum. Most **Equity Funds** ku, **3:00 PM** dhaan cut-off.

  • Before 3:00 PM (and Money Received): Neenga **sariyaana time** la order panni, **payment** kooda **sariyaana time** la received aagiduchi na, **adhai naal oda NAV** dhaan kidaikkum.
  • After 3:00 PM: Neenga **3:00 PM ku appuram** order kudutha, **next working day** oda **NAV** dhaan kidaikkum.

Why NAV Timing is Impossible

NAV ku **time panna** (timing the market) **try panna koodadhu** da. Yenendraal, neenga order kudukkum bodhu, **adhai naal** oda **final NAV** evalo nu **yaarum predict panna mudiyadhu**. Idhudhaan **SIP discipline** ku romba mukkiyam.
